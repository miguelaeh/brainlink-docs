## What's BrainLink

BrainLink is a global AI account with access to the all models available in the market. You can connect your BrainLink account to any AI application with one-click and pay exactly
for what you consume.

In other words, we are the first AI utility provider. Just like electricity, where you hire a provider and connect all your appliances to it, you can connect
all your AI applications to BrainLink and have a single source to pay and track your consumption.

You can see and control your global AI spending per application on the BrainLink dashboard.
